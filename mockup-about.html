Our story

Created to service the growing need for specialist ADAC and As Constructed providers

ADACT was created to provide independent, specialist ADAC and as constructed assistance. Specialising in ADAC files, as constructed plans and civil engineering drafting. Based on the Sunshine Coast, we provide drafting services and ADAC files for projects across Australia - assisting with submissions to all local councils and water utility providers across the full range of civil assets.

Behind ADACT

Founded by Lee Goldsmith

ADACT is led by Lee Goldsmith, bringing over a decade of practical experience across civil engineering, project management, ADAC and As Constructed services, drafting and civil asset management. This background allows ADACT to understand project requirements from design through to handover, helping clients prepare accurate, authority-ready documentation with industry-leading turnaround times.
